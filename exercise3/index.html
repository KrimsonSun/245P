The Four Elements

Roots in the Bronx, NYC.

Hip Hop culture, founded by DJ Kool Herc, rests on four main elements. These are DJing (mixing music), MCing (rapping), B-boying (breakdancing), and Graffiti (visual art). These pillars define the culture's unique expression.

The Golden Era

Innovation and growth.

This period is considered Hip Hop's peak in creativity and influence. It introduced diverse sub-genres and socially conscious lyrics. Artists like Public Enemy and Nas dominated this highly impactful era in music history.
