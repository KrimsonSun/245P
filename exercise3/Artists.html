Nas

The Lyrical Messiah

A cornerstone of the 1990s "Golden Era," Nas cemented his legendary status with his debut album, *Illmatic*, at just 20 years old. Known for his dense, complex, and poetic rhymes, Nas provides panoramic views of street life, philosophical introspection, and the black experience in America, setting a high standard for lyrical artistry.

Kendrick Lamar

The Voice of a Generation

Hailing from Compton, California, Kendrick Lamar is widely regarded as one of the most influential rappers of the 21st century. His albums, such as *To Pimp a Butterfly* and *DAMN.*, weave intricate narratives addressing socio-political issues, racial identity, and personal struggle, earning him a Pulitzer Prize in 2018.
